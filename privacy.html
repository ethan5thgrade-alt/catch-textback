Catch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Catch is a missed-call text-back service operated by Ethan Strauss ("Catch," "we," "us"). When you call a local service business that uses Catch and your call isn't answered, Catch sends an automated text from that business so they can follow up with you. This policy explains what we collect and how we handle it, including your mobile information.

Information we collect

Your mobile phone number, captured when you place a call to a business that uses Catch; the content of text messages you exchange with the business (for example, the service you need, your address, and scheduling details); and basic delivery information such as timestamps and delivery status.

How we use it

We use your number and messages only to reply to your inquiry, help the business follow up, and schedule or complete the work you contacted them about.

How we share it — we do not sell or share for marketing

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or any opt-in or consent information with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information is shared only with the service providers (such as our messaging carrier, Twilio) needed to deliver the messages you requested, and never for their own marketing.

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies and depends on your interaction with the business — typically you receive messages only after you call them. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out and help

Reply STOP to any message to opt out and stop receiving texts. Reply HELP for help. You can also contact us at ethan5thgrade@gmail.com.

Data retention

We keep messages and contact details only as long as needed to provide the service and as required by law.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email ethan5thgrade@gmail.com.
