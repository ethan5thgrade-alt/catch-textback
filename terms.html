Catch — Terms of Service

Last updated: June 21, 2026

These terms govern text messages sent through Catch, a missed-call text-back service operated by Ethan Strauss.

The service

When you call a participating local business and the call isn't answered, the business's number sends you an automated text so they can follow up. Continuing the conversation by text is optional.

Consent

By calling a participating business, you may receive a reply text from that business. You can stop at any time by replying STOP.

Message frequency and charges

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the business. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile plan.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help, or email ethan5thgrade@gmail.com.
